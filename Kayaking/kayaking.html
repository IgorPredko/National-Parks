Kayaking
Explore new horizons

Choice of single or double kayak, 30 minute dockside instruction prior to departure customized to your experience level. Topics covered include: how to adjust your seat and foot pedals, how to use your rudder, basic paddle strokes, stability, how to wet exit, and how to perform an assisted-rescue. Tour guides that are passionate and keen-eyed naturalists who are exceptional at finding wildlife and eager to share their wealth of knowledge about the local flora, fauna, and natural history of the area.
